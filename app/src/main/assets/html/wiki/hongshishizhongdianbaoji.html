这是一种使用红石时钟的电报机，以记录和储存数据。因此让我们开始制作它吧。

  1. 首先你需要建造一座建筑物，至少尺寸为16×5方块，还要让它看起来像一个邮局或者某些东西。
  2. 接下来，在墙上挖一个长洞。它必须在16×5方块的建筑里长12个方块（如果你把建筑物建的更大，要在墙上留4个方块或在其它方块留空间）你必须有4个方块的闲置空间。
  3. 沿着洞放置全延迟的红石中继器。
  4. Hook up to a redstone clock (the bigger the better). A good way to save space is to go up down up down in rows.
  5. That will be the storer.
  6. Next is the Sender, place a lever on one of the spaces. It's good to have a sign on top saying Sender.
  7. After that, make a line of repeaters on full delay to the other telegraph office (A good way to do this is to go block repeater block so on) to make a turn place a block then, place a repeater facing the towards the place. Hook up to redstone the clock on the other one.
  8. To use the Sender do it in Morse code, To make a Dot, turn on then off as fast you can, To make a Dash turn On count to three then off again.
  9. Next is the Deleter, Place blocks in the clock with sticky pistons touching them when on so you can take them out of the clock stopping the pulse (Make it so it won't break things just take the block out)
  10. Now the Canceller, Let's say you were sending a telegram and you made a mistake, Oh no how do I stop it, just make a deleter, good to make lots up the track, as with the Clock.

That's it all done

优点：

  • Can leave messages.
  • Fast.
  • Easy to use.

缺点：

  • Needs lots of repeaters.
  • Morse code Needed.
  • Lags a fair bit (Good way to stop this is to keep it well lit in the redstone clock.)
  • Needs a large Clock to do 7 letters word.

小贴士：

  • Good to have a Morse code up some where.
  • Write it out on paper.
  • Place signs on top of levers
  • Nice touch is to hook note blocks up to the line, not the clock.
  • Make more clocks to take more messages.
